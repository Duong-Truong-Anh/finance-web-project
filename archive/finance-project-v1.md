# **PRACTICAL ASSIGNMENT**

## **Using AI Software to Build a Cash Flow Management Website and Simulate Long-Term Stock Investment**

### **1\. Assignment Objective**

Students are required to use **AI software** to support the development of a **simple personal/business financial management website** with the following functions:

* Record **monthly cash inflows**.  
* Record **monthly cash outflows**.  
* Calculate **monthly net cash flow** using the following formula:

Monthly Net Cash Flow \= Total Monthly Inflows \- Total Monthly Outflows

* Assume that during the **first 5 years**, the proportion of **net cash flow allocated for investment** ranges from **30% to 50%**, with this ratio being **customizable by the user**.  
* The monthly investment amount will be **equally allocated** to a portfolio of **5 stock codes**.  
* Assume that these stocks generate an average annual growth rate of **15%–20% per year**.  
* The website must **simulate the future investment value** after **10 years, 20 years, and 30 years**.  
* The website must also create **statistical charts** to illustrate the growth of the investment over time.

---

### **2\. Implementation Requirements**

Students **must use AI software** to support the process of designing, coding, and completing the website.  
They may use AI tools such as:

* ChatGPT  
* Gemini  
* Copilot  
* Claude  
* or other similar AI software

Students are expected to use AI to:

* Generate website design ideas  
* Support coding  
* Help debug errors  
* Assist in building the interface  
* Support chart creation and data simulation

However, students must:

* Clearly understand the work they have done  
* Be able to explain the source code and the logic of how the website works  
* Take responsibility for the accuracy of the final results

---

### **3\. Functional Requirements of the Website**

#### **3.1. Income Management**

The website must allow users to:

* Enter monthly income items  
* Store the following information:  
  * Income item name  
  * Amount  
  * Date/month of record  
  * Notes (if any)

#### **3.2. Expense Management**

The website must allow users to:

* Enter monthly expense items  
* Store the following information:  
  * Expense item name  
  * Amount  
  * Date/month of record  
  * Notes (if any)

#### **3.3. Monthly Net Cash Flow Calculation**

The system must automatically calculate:

* Total monthly income  
* Total monthly expenses  
* Monthly net cash flow

The results must be displayed in the form of:

* A monthly data table  
* Visual charts

---

### **4\. Investment Simulation**

* During the **first 5 years**, users can choose an investment ratio from **30% to 50%** of monthly net cash flow.  
* The monthly investment amount will be **equally distributed among 5 stock codes**.  
* The stocks in the portfolio are assumed to have an average annual growth rate of **15%–20%**.  
* The website must calculate and simulate the investment portfolio value after:  
  * **10 years**  
  * **20 years**  
  * **30 years**

---

### **5\. Expected Output**

The website must display:

* Monthly net cash flow  
* Monthly investment amount  
* Amount allocated to each stock code  
* Portfolio value at the milestones of **10 years, 20 years, and 30 years**  
* Statistical charts showing the growth of the investment portfolio

---

### **6\. Report Requirements**

Students are required to submit:

* The website source code  
* A report describing:  
  * The AI software used  
  * How AI was used during the assignment  
  * The main functions of the website  
  * The method for calculating net cash flow  
  * The method for simulating investment growth  
* Screenshots of the product  
* A short demo video (if available)

---

### **7\. Important Notes**

* AI is only a **supporting tool** and does not completely replace the role of the student.  
* Students must **understand, verify, and revise** any content generated by AI.  
* The instructor may require students to **directly explain** the website structure, calculation formulas, and source code used.
